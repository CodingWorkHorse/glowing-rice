About Us

He made it his life goal to find the perfect recipe and make the Italian classic even better. Using old world secrets with new world techniques, he has reached his goal and made the perfect gelato. How We Started

Our company was founded on the basis that just because we are on a different continent does not mean that we need not the best product available. We set out to show the American people what hard work and good craftsmanship together can produce. When Dillon went on a trip to the beautiful country of Italy he realized that here we did not have as rigorous standards for gelato they did there. When Dillon came home he realized that he needed to show the American people a higher caliber of gelato. He bought a gelato maker one night and mixed ingredients until he came up with the perfect combo. From that he started his company to help him pursue his dream of making the worlds best gelato.
