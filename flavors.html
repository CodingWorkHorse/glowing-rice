Mint Chip

The word mint comes from the latin word mentha which in turn came from the name of the Greek nymph Minthe, who according to the myth was turned into the mint plant after trying to seduce Hades, one of the most important Greek gods. Mint has always been an important plant, and has a long history to it. Originally used as an aid to chest and stomach pains, mint was used by the Greeks in there baths to help stimulate there bodies. It was than used by the Romans in their sauces, and as a mouth wash. The monks of old, also used mint for its many culinary properties. Mint is still very widely used, and is a favorite herb of many.

When we make our mint chip gelato, we follow our rule of using the least amount of ingredients possible to give you the freshest most natural gelato we can. We use absolutely no coloring what so ever in Mint Chip. That is why our mint chip is a natural color, and not some radioactive green. We believe in the real stuff, and our dedicated to producing the best gelato we can. We hope you love our mint gelato as much as we do. Buon Appetito!

Madagascar Vanilla

The word Vanilla coming from the Spanish word "vaina" meaning pod, has been enjoyed for hundreds of years. First enjoyed by the Totonacs of Eastern Mexico, and than by the Aztecs, which took them over, Vanilla has always been a treat to enjoy. The Aztecs enjoyed it in their drink "Chocolatl" Which was mostly chocolate, but with added vanilla for even more taste. When Vanilla, got over to Europe later, it was instantly enjoyed there too. Vanilla though, didn't truly separate from chocolate until the 17th century, when the Queen of England, Queen Elizabeth 1st, ordered an all vanilla sweetener, when she tried it she fell in love, and within the next century it spread all over Europe, and quickly became a classic gelato flavor.
